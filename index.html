Animated Ring Timer

A smooth circular progress ring counts down your session. Three modes: Focus (25 min), Short Break (5 min), Long Break (15 min).

Background Persistence

The timer keeps running even when the popup is closed. Switch tabs, browse freely — the countdown continues silently.

Session Analytics

Track total sessions completed, total minutes focused, and your running streak — all visible in the popup at a glance.

30-Day History

A dedicated History tab shows sessions and minutes per day for the last 30 days so you can see your study patterns over time.

Weekly Streak Calendar

A seven-dot weekly calendar marks every day you completed at least one session. Build the chain — do not break it.

Gentle Completion Sound

A soft three-note chime plays when each session ends — pleasant enough to notice, quiet enough not to break your flow.

Simple setup

Running in under a minute

No developer tools required. Four steps and you are done.

1

Download

Click the download button and save the extension file to your computer.

2

Open Cent Browser

StudyBloom installs in Cent Browser, a free Chromium-based browser available for Windows and Mac.

3

Add Extension

Open the downloaded file in Cent Browser and click Add Extension when prompted.

4

Start Studying

Pin StudyBloom to your toolbar, click it, press Start, and begin your first session.
